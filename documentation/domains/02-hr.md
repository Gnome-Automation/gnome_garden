# People and Affiliations

**Implemented Domain:** `GnomeGarden.Operations`
**Purpose:** Durable people records and their relationship to organizations

This file keeps the old `02-hr` slot, but there is no standalone HR Ash domain today. Human records live in `Operations`, with user identity in `Accounts`.

## Resources

### `Person`
Represents an internal or external human record.

### `OrganizationAffiliation`
Represents the relationship between a person and an organization.

## Current Scope

Implemented today:
- person records
- company/person affiliation records
- linking external requesters and contacts into commercial and service flows

Not implemented as a standalone HR system:
- internal employee master data
- payroll
- certification tracking
- formal skills/capacity planning

## How It Fits

The current platform uses people records mainly for:
- external contacts tied to organizations
- service requesters
- commercial/contact context
- future internal staffing and assignment expansion

## Long-Term Direction

If a dedicated HR or staffing model is added later, it should build on:
- `Accounts.User`
- `Operations.Person`
- `Operations.OrganizationAffiliation`

rather than introducing a second competing person model.
